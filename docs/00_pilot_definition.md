# Pilot Definition - Maroa Punta Cana (v0.1)

**Horizon:** 4 weeks | **Focus:** Validate, not scale | **Operation:** Concierge-first

---

## 1. Objective

Validate demand, operations, and unit economics for A/C maintenance services in Punta Cana before scaling.

## 2. Scope

| Dimension | In Scope | Out of Scope |
|-----------|----------|--------------|
| **Geography** | Ciudad La Palma (principal), Punta Cana Village/Resort, Cap Cana (secundarios) | Bavaro, Veron, Uvero Alto, otras zonas |
| **Service Type** | A/C preventive maintenance, minor correctives | Major repairs, installations, other appliances |
| **Clients** | Residential homes (1-4 A/C units) | Commercial, industrial |
| **Channels** | WhatsApp Business, referrals, community groups | Paid ads, landing pages |
| **Tools** | WhatsApp + Google Forms + Google Sheets | Custom app, CRM software |

## 3. Hypotheses to Validate

| ID | Hypothesis | Key Metric | IMM Rationale |
|----|------------|------------|---------------|
| H1 | Clients will pay for subscription/one-time A/C maintenance | Conversion rate >=20% | Problem statement + market sizing SOM ~0.61M USD/yr (imm-doulab/01, 03) |
| H2 | Concierge operation can deliver "zero drama" experience | SLA compliance >=90% | Transforming purpose + concierge model as solution (imm-doulab/04, 05) |
| H3 | Technicians can deliver consistent quality | First-time fix rate >=90% | OKR O2 + technician scoring unique advantage (imm-doulab/03, 04) |
| H4 | Unit economics are viable | Gross margin >=20% | Subscription experiment designed in IMM (imm-doulab/08) |
| H5 | Clients will recommend and return | Retention intent >=60% | OKR O1 "preventive on autopilot" + "cero drama" promise (imm-doulab/03, 06) |

## 4. Target Volume

- **Leads:** 30+ qualified
- **Paid services:** 15-20
- **Active technicians:** 2 + 1 backup

## 5. Success Criteria (Go/Pivot/Stop)

| Result | Criteria | Action |
|--------|----------|--------|
| **Go** | All H1-H5 meet minimum thresholds | Prepare phase 2, expand volume |
| **Pivot** | 1-2 hypotheses below threshold | Adjust offer/operation, re-test 7 days |
| **Stop** | >2 hypotheses fail or critical incident | Pause, redesign fundamentals |

## 6. Constraints

- No custom app development during pilot
- All operations via WhatsApp + Sheets
- Maximum 20 services to control quality
- Single geographic zone

## 8. Strategic Foundation (IMM Research)

This pilot is grounded in the customer discovery and strategy work from the IMM Doulab Phase 02 innovation program (Q2 2025). See `imm-doulab/README.md` for the full program index.

### Customer Segments Identified

| Segment | Profile | Key Behavior |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Homeowners | Age 30-55, US$1,500-4,000/mo, 4-8 assets | Call technicians by referral, forget maintenance, wait for failure |
| Airbnb hosts | Age 28-50, 2-5 properties, 10-20 assets | Need fast response, 24/7 availability, accounting records |
| Small businesses | Age 30-60, US$2,000-8,000/mo, 5-15 critical assets | Equipment failures directly impact operations and revenue |
| Expats | Age 35-65, US$3,000-10,000/mo, 5-10 assets | Prefer digital processes, secure payments, trust |
| Informal technicians | Age 22-50, US$500-1,500/mo, limited tools | Manage via WhatsApp, unstable income |

**Pilot focus:** Segment 1 (homeowners with 1-4 A/C units) in Punta Cana.

### Problem Statement (from IMM)

> Los propietarios de vivienda, pequenos negocios y propietarios de rentas cortas en la Republica Dominicana no pueden mantener sus equipos criticos en optimas condiciones de forma preventiva porque no existe un servicio confiable, coordinado y accesible que conecte la demanda con tecnicos calificados, lo que resulta en altos costos por reparaciones de emergencia, equipos con vida util reducida y estres operativo constante.

### Transforming Purpose

> Existimos para empoderar a los hogares, negocios y tecnicos de la Republica Dominicana a vivir con tranquilidad y confianza, transformando la cultura reactiva del mantenimiento en una experiencia preventiva, organizada y de excelencia.

### Core Message

**"Maroa te da tranquilidad cero drama con tus aires."**
