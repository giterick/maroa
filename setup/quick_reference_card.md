## SERVICE FLOW (8 Steps)

```
1. LEAD     → New inquiry comes in
2. INTAKE   → Qualify (zone, units, needs)
3. TRIAGE   → Fit? → Schedule or decline
4. SCHEDULE → Book date/time with tech
5. CONFIRM  → T-24h and T-2h reminders
6. EXECUTE  → Tech does service + photos
7. CLOSE    → Payment + thank you
8. FOLLOW   → Satisfaction + referral ask
```
